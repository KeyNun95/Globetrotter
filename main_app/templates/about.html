We are the Tailblazers

Mission Statement

Streamline travel planning with a user friendly platform that allows all your planning needs to be met via 1 App: Globetrotter!

Vision Satement

Planning a trip can be complicated and time-consuming, especially when its with a group. Our vision is to simplify the art of travel planning by empowering adventurers to effortlessly create, organize, and share personalized itineraries.
